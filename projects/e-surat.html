E-Surat

  • Tech Stack: PHP Codeigniter, SweetAlert, MySQL, REST API, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Intregate with web service (API Mercu Buana University), Mobile responsive, integrated with email notification using Send Email.
  • URL: https://https://e-surat.mercubuana.ac.id

This application serves as the administrative platform for Mercu Buana University's faculty. It is specifically designed for use by students at Mercu Buana University, allowing them to submit letters of introduction from the campus. These letters are typically required when students seek internships or wish to conduct research in a company. The primary purpose of the application is to streamline and facilitate the process for students who are interested in engaging in internships or research activities by providing a convenient and efficient means of submitting the necessary documentation.
