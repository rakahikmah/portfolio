Generasi Maju

  • Tech Stack:Pimcore (Content Management System), PHP Symfony, MySQL, Jquery, Ajax, Using Jira
  • URL: https://www.generasimaju.co.id/

This application is a company profile platform developed for one of the programs organized by generasimaju.co.id. Through this application, users can find various articles focusing on the health of children and pregnant women. This health information covers relevant and beneficial topics for those concerned about the growth and development of children, as well as the well-being of pregnant mothers.

Furthermore, the application also includes information about the sale of milk products. Users can easily access health-related articles while also having access to information and offers related to the sale of milk products. Thus, the application aims to provide a comprehensive and informative resource for users interested in topics related to the health of children, pregnant women, and milk products.
