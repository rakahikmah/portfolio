Merchant Ops Portal

  • Tech Stack: ReactJS, Redux Thunk, SweetAlert, Jenkins, IronPDF, Axios, REST API, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, using Jira

This application is designed with the primary goal of verifying the data of merchants who have registered through our official registration platform, namely https://merchant.cashlez.com. The verification process is carried out meticulously by the Cashlez operational team and the risk team to ensure that the data submitted by the merchants meets the established standards of validity and security.

After passing through the careful verification stage and the merchant's data is deemed valid, the Cashlez operational team provides approval. Subsequently, the merchant is entitled to access and use the payment features provided through the dedicated payment gateway owned by Cashlez. This process is not only a verification step but also serves as an entry point for merchants to enjoy all the ease and benefits offered by Cashlez's payment services.
